Which roadway segments are missing full sidwalk coverage?

This map shows the results of the sidewalk coverage analysis for the NJ side of the DVRPC region. The analysis included the following steps for each centerline:

  1. Calculate the length of parallel sidewalks from the regional sidewalk inventory
  2. Divide #1 by the centerline length
  3. Divide #2 by 2 to account for sidewalks being on both sides of the street
  4. Classify #3 from a decimal number into text

  • RED - no sidewalks (0.0)
  • ORANGE - partial sidewalk on one side only (>0.0 & <0.4)
  • YELLOW - continuous on one side, partial on the other (>=0.4 & <0.8)
  • GREEN - full sidewalk coverage (>=0.8)
