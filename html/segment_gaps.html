Which roadway segments are missing full sidwalk coverage?

This map shows the results of a sidewalk coverage analysis for the DVRPC region. For each road centerline the length of parallel sidewalks was calculated. This value was divided by the centerline's length, and then by 2, assuming that sidewalks should be on both sides of the street. The resulting numeric "sidewalk coverage" ratio is converted to one of the classifications shown in the legend and displayed with color on this map.

Zoom in on an area of interest and the sidewalk layer will appear, allowing detailed review of the data inputs and analysis results.
