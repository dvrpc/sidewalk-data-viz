How similar are the sidewalk- and centerline-walksheds around rail stations?

This map shows the results of a network routing analysis for all rail stations in the DVRPC region, comparing the areas for each station reachable on the sidewalk network alone and the full OpenStreetMap (centerline) network.

The station colors reflect the ratio between these two walksheds:

  • Green: walksheds are the same size
  • Yellow: OpenStreetMap walkshed is roughly twice the size of the sidewalk walkshed
  • Red: The sidewalk walkshed is tiny or does not exist at all

Clicking on a rail station point will show the two walksheds for the selected station:

  • The sidewalk-based walkshed is shown in green
  • The OpenStreetMap-based walkshed is shown in gray
