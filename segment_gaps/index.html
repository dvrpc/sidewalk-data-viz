About

This map contains energy use and greenhouse gas emissions profiles for municipalities in Greater Philadelphia. The data within the profiles are from DVRPC's 2015 Energy Use and Greenhouse Gas Emissions in Greater Philadelphia. To learn more about the methodology behind these profiles and the greater inventory please see Energy Use and Greenhouse Gas Emissions Inventory for Greater Philadelphia: Methods and Sources.

Hover over a municipality to see its name. Click within the municipality to download the report.
